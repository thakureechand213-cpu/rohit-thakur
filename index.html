The Story

Crafted with passion,
served with soul

Rohit Thakur is an experienced Chinese Chef with over 4 years of expertise in professional kitchens, specialising in Cantonese, Sichuan, and Hakka cuisines. Adept at preparing a wide range of authentic Chinese dishes — from delicate dim sum and silken broths to fiery Sichuan mains and comforting Hakka noodles.

Known for creativity, exceptional teamwork, and unwavering dedication to high kitchen standards, Rohit brings a combination of technical skill and artistic flair to every dish. His passion for food extends beyond the kitchen — he loves exploring new places, photography, and continuously expanding his culinary horizons.
